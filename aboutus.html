JMU Smart Spots is a project aimed to allow students an easy way of finding rooms, tables, or desks available in a given spot. The project is split into three groups:front end, back end, and center. The center team is responsoble for datamanagement, the back end team is responsible for all hardware, and the front end team is resposible for data to user experience. Hopeful features for the front end website are: the ability to see a list of available spots in real time, the ability to reserve a spot for selected times from the list, and ability to download the app version from the website.
